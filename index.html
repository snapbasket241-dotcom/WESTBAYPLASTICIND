About Us

West Bay Plastic Ind is a Vapi-based recycling and trading company incorporated in 2020. We are a constantly growing company in the recycling industry. Our establishment was principally designed to manage scrap in a professional manner — maximizing recycling and minimizing waste for disposal, helping our customers avoid unnecessary costs and, whenever possible, increase revenues.


Our company is managed and staffed by a group of professionals with vast experience in the recycling industry, ensuring quality, efficiency, and reliability in every engagement.
